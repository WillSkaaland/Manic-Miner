Manic Mining

How to play?
The electronic luxuries enjoyed by many are a direct result of child labor in the Democratic Republic of congo. Both children and adults are paid little, often unable to fully pay for all their costs of living , picking and choosing what they have to purchase. Electric vehicles are praised for their environmentally friendly nature, but getting these vehicles produced are all but a struggle for those less fortunate It’s time for you to experience this labor, in an easier, virtual way. Attempt to mine out all 1000 tiles, taking ten clicks to fully mine a tile, and find whatever cobalt you can. At least you don’t have to brave blistering heat with very little water.
